About Me

My name is Enock Nyakaana, a passionate filmmaker and visual storyteller. I am currently a second-year student at Kampala Film School, pursuing my dream of building a strong career in the film industry.

I directed Charcoal and Courage, a film that received nominations at the iKon Awards and the Uganda Film Festival, a milestone that strengthened my vision of creating impactful stories that reflect society, culture, and resilience.

I am 22 years old, proudly born and raised in Uganda, and deeply inspired by the power of film to shape perspectives, inspire change, and tell authentic African stories.
